About this website

In the last year, I've been looking at how website performance and loading resources affect accessibility and sustainability. Heavier loads on performance can exclude people with lower data budgets and less reliable internet connections.

They also use more electricity.

As an experiment, I have built this site to be as lean as possible without images, frameworks or libraries. I have set the typeface to sans serif for readability. There are no custom typefaces or weights to download.

I love bright colours, especially yellow and green. So I have used a gradient to represent my personality, rather than a photo of myself.
