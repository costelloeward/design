These are two highlights from my coding studies in the last year. There is more information about my background in other disciplines on my LinkedIn profile.

Creative Dice

I write as a hobby and some of my friends are in writers groups. We have found creative dice useful as a writing prompt, but wanted to move away from relying on physical dice.

I searched for creative dice online, the sites I found had dice diagrams that were printed out and then folded.

This was not the remote version my friends and I needed. Some of us have dexterity issues with folding or do not have a printer.

So I drew the pictures in Paint and built online creative dice myself.
